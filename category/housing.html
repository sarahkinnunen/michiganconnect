Housing Resources

Emergency shelter, transitional housing, rental assistance, and homelessness prevention

How to Access Housing Services

Call First

Most shelters and housing programs require a call before arriving. Availability changes daily - always call ahead to confirm a bed or appointment.

What to Bring

Bring a photo ID if you have one. Many programs accept you without ID. Having proof of income or an eviction notice can speed up the intake process.

Need Help Right Now?

If you are on the street or in immediate danger, go to the Find Help Now page for emergency contacts.
