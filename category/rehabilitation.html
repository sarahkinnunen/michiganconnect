Substance use recovery programs, mental health rehabilitation, and peer support services for individuals and families

Getting Started with Recovery Support

You Don't Have to Do It Alone

Reaching out is the hardest step. Recovery programs are confidential and judgment-free. A counselor or peer support specialist can help you figure out the right next steps – you do not need to have everything figured out before you call.

Know Your Options

Programs range from outpatient counseling and peer support groups to residential treatment and medically supervised detox. Different pathways work for different people. Ask about all available options when you make first contact with an organization.

Insurance & Cost

Many programs accept Medicaid and offer sliding-scale fees based on income. Do not let cost stop you from reaching out – most organizations will work with you to find a payment solution. If you need Medicaid, see our Medicaid guide.
