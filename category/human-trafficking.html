Human Trafficking Resources

Hotlines, safe housing, legal advocacy, and survivor support services

Getting Help Safely

National Hotline

Call or text the National Human Trafficking Hotline at 1-888-373-7888 or text 233733 (HELP or INFO). Available 24/7, confidential, and available in 200+ languages.

Your Safety Comes First

If it is not safe to call, text or use the online chat options. Most services are confidential. You do not need to have left your situation to ask for help.

Reporting Concerns

If you suspect trafficking is happening, contact the National Hotline or local law enforcement. You do not need proof — trained advocates will guide next steps.
