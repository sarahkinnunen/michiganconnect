Getting Started with Parenting Support Programs

Know What You Need

Parenting support ranges from diaper banks and parenting classes to intensive in-home family support. Knowing what type of help you are looking for will help you connect with the right program faster.

Bring Your Child's Info

Many programs ask for your child's age, school enrollment status, and medical information. Having a copy of your child's birth certificate and immunization records can also speed up enrollment in some services.

Income Requirements Vary

Some programs are open to all parents and caregivers. Others are income-based or require a referral. Always ask when you call – many organizations can connect you to other help if they cannot serve you directly.
