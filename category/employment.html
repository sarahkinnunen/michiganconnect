Employment Resources

Job training, workforce programs, summer employment, and career placement for youth and young adults

Getting Started with Employment Programs

Apply Early

Many programs like summer employment have application windows that close months in advance. Check program websites early and apply as soon as applications open.

What to Bring

Most programs require a photo ID and proof of address. Some may ask for a Social Security card, school records, or proof of income. Call ahead to confirm.

Training + Jobs

Some programs combine job training, GED prep, and paid work in one package. Look at both employment and education resources if you are still working toward a diploma.
