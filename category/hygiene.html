Hygiene & Wellness Services

Free showers, laundry facilities, hygiene kits, and personal care items for people in need

How to Access Hygiene Services

Walk-Ins Welcome

Most hygiene programs allow walk-ins during open hours. Arrive early as same-day slots for showers or laundry can fill up quickly, especially in the morning.

What to Expect

Many programs offer showers with towels provided, coin-free laundry, hygiene kits (soap, shampoo, toothbrush, toothpaste, deodorant), and sometimes a change of clothing. Ask what is available when you arrive.

No ID Required

Most hygiene service programs do not require a photo ID or proof of address to use showers or receive hygiene supplies. Just show up during open hours. Some comprehensive service centers may ask for basic information for their records.
