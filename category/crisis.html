About Crisis Services

📞 Crisis Hotlines

Free, confidential phone or text lines staffed by trained counselors. Available 24/7. No ID or insurance needed. You can hang up at any time.

🚪 Drop-In Centers

Safe, welcoming spaces where youth can walk in during posted hours. No appointment needed. Some offer food, showers, and referrals.

🔁 Ongoing Support

Many crisis organizations also offer case management, counseling, and long-term support. Tell them what you need — they can help you find next steps.
