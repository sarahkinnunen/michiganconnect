Healthcare Resources

Free and low-cost clinics, mental health services, dental care, and public health programs

How to Access Healthcare

No Insurance? No Problem

Many clinics offer sliding scale fees based on your income, including free care for those who qualify. Always ask about uninsured patient programs when you call.

What to Bring

Bring any existing insurance cards, a photo ID if you have one, and a list of current medications. Most clinics can assist you even if you lack documentation.

Mental Health Counts

Mental health is healthcare. Many clinics below offer counseling, therapy, and psychiatric services. You don't need a crisis to ask for help.
