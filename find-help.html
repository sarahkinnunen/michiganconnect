🆘 Find Help Now

This page is for people who need immediate help. Every resource listed below is available now. You do not need ID, insurance, or money to call.

☎️ Call or Text Right Now

Any Emergency
911
Police, fire, or medical emergency. Available 24/7.
Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
988
Call or text 988 anytime. Free, confidential. Available 24/7 in English & Spanish.
United Way 211
211
Dial or text 211 to be connected to local resources for any need. Available 24/7.
National Runaway Safeline
1-800-786-2929
For runaway and at-risk youth up to age 21. Call, text, or chat 24/7. No name required.
Crisis Text Line
Text HOME to 741741
Free, confidential crisis counseling by text. Available 24/7.
Domestic Violence Hotline
1-800-799-7233
National DV hotline. Call, text, or chat at thehotline.org. Available 24/7.
