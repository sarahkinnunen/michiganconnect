What to Expect When You Call

  • You don't need ID to call a crisis line or access most emergency services.
  • Your call is confidential. What you share stays with the counselor.
  • No judgment. Crisis counselors are trained to listen and help without judging.
  • You can hang up at any time. There's no obligation.
  • Language access is available on most hotlines - tell them your language.
