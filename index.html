Learning Center

Free, step-by-step guides to help you apply for benefits, get documents, and build stability

Benefits & Assistance

Walk through applying for Medicaid, SNAP (food stamps), SSI/SSDI disability, TANF cash assistance, and WIC — with step-by-step instructions and direct form links.

Housing

Learn how to apply for Section 8 / Housing Choice Vouchers and emergency rental assistance programs, including what documents you'll need and what to expect.

Identity & Legal Docs

Get a Michigan driver's license or state ID, request a birth certificate, apply for naturalization (N-400), or renew DACA (I-821D) — all explained in plain language.

Employment & Education

Build a resume, file for unemployment benefits, enroll in GED programs, or apply to Detroit Job Corps — with timelines, requirements, and links to get started.
