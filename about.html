About The Connect

Our mission, our values, and our commitment to transparency.

Our Mission

The Connect exists to close the gap between people who need help and the organizations that provide it. We serve youth and families across Wayne and Monroe counties — with a focus on removing barriers like complex language, confusing eligibility rules, and outdated information.

We believe that no one should go without housing, food, healthcare, or safety because they didn't know where to look. This site is built to be simple, fast, and trustworthy — designed for people under real stress, who need real answers, right now.

Resources listed here are regularly verified by our team. We contact each organization to confirm current hours, eligibility, and contact information. Every resource card shows a "Last Verified" date so you can trust what you see.

Our Values

🎯

Accuracy

Every resource is manually verified. We list a "Last Verified" date on every card so you always know how current the information is.

♿

Accessibility

Built WCAG-conscious from the ground up. High contrast, keyboard navigable, screen-reader friendly, mobile-first. This site should work for everyone.

🔒

Privacy

We do not collect personal data, require sign-up, or track individual searches. This site exists to help, not to harvest information.

🤝

Community

We work with local organizations, advocates, and community members to keep this directory useful and relevant. Anyone can suggest a resource.

📖

Plain Language

We write for a 6th-grade reading level. No jargon. Clear instructions. If it's confusing, we rewrite it.

🌱

Growth

We started with Wayne and Monroe counties and will expand geographically as capacity grows. The data architecture is built to scale.

Data & Verification Policy

Resource data is stored in a structured JSON file and updated manually by our team. Each resource record includes a last_verified_date field. Our goal is to verify each resource at least every 90 days.

Verification means we have confirmed the organization's name, phone number, address, hours, and eligibility via direct contact or their official website.

If you find outdated or incorrect information, please let us know immediately. We take data accuracy seriously — lives may depend on this information being correct.

Accessibility Statement

The Connect is committed to digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines. Our site is designed to be:

  • Fully keyboard navigable (use Tab, Enter, Space, Arrow keys)
  • Screen reader compatible (semantic HTML, ARIA labels, live regions)
  • High contrast (meets or exceeds 4.5:1 color contrast ratio)
  • Zoomable up to 200% without loss of functionality
  • Operable with reduced motion settings
  • Mobile and touch accessible (minimum 44×44px tap targets)

If you experience an accessibility barrier, please contact us and we will work to address it promptly.
