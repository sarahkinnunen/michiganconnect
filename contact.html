Contact & Suggest a Resource

Have a question, found incorrect information, or want to add a resource? We want to hear from you.

Contact forms

Need help right now? This is not a crisis line. If you need immediate help, please visit Find Help Now or call 211 or 988.

Send Us a Message

Be as specific as possible. If reporting incorrect info, include the resource name.

Suggest a Resource

Know of a local organization serving Wayne or Monroe counties that isn't listed here? Fill out what you know and we'll verify and add it.

The more detail you provide, the faster we can verify and add the resource.

What Happens Next

  1. We receive your submission and review it within 3–5 business days.
  2. If you suggested a resource, we contact the organization to verify details.
  3. Once verified, we add the resource to the directory.
  4. If you included your email, we'll let you know when it goes live.

We do not sell or share your contact information. See our Data Policy.
