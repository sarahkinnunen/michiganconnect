How to Access These Services

Loading guidance…

Having ID (even an expired one), proof of address, and any relevant paperwork can help speed up the process. However, most services will help you even without documentation. Don't let missing documents stop you from reaching out.

If one resource can't serve you, ask them for a referral. You can also call 211 or browse the full directory to find alternatives. Never give up — help exists.
